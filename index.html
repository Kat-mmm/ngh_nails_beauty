About Us

Naledi's Glam House is a premier beauty destination that offers a full range of luxurious nail services for both men and women. Our talented and certified nail technicians, led by founder Naledi Hokoane, are dedicated to delivering exceptional beauty treatments that will leave you feeling refreshed, rejuvenated and glamourous. With a passion for all things nails and a drive for delivering affordable luxury, Naledi's Glam House is the perfect place to relax, unwind and indulge in a little pampering. Whether you're looking for a simple manicure or an intricate nail design, we have everything you need to bring out the best in your hands and feet. Let us help you shine and embrace your inner glamour at Naledi's Glam House.
